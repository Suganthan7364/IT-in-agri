Unit V - E-Governance in Agricultural Systems

Expert Systems and Decision Support Systems

Expert systems and decision support systems in agriculture help farmers make data-driven decisions by analyzing patterns, predicting outcomes, and offering solutions for better resource management.

Agricultural and Biological Databases

Databases for agriculture and biology store vast amounts of information that support research, policy-making, and decision-making in farming practices.

E-Commerce and E-Business Systems

E-commerce and e-business systems enable farmers to access markets directly, sell their products online, and adopt efficient supply chain management practices.

Technology Enhanced Learning Systems

Technology-enhanced learning systems provide farmers and rural communities with access to knowledge and training, fostering innovation and development.

Rural Development and Information Society

The integration of information technology into rural development empowers communities, enhances communication, and bridges the digital divide in agriculture.
